iSober

A web app to to track how long you'll be artificially happy.

How Long?

Stone Cold? Getting There? Buzzed? How long till you need another drink?

Configurable

Pick your weight, and desired BAC, to know when it's time to enjoy.

Social

Take a picture and post every drink to Facebook so your friends know you're having a good night.

Open

It's a one-page app using HTML/JS.

The project is available on Github so why not contribute?
